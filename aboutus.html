About Us


At Holiday Inn, we believe in nurturing a personal connection between the brand, the staff and most importantly, our beloved guests. Mutual respect shared among each of these entities translates into seamless communication, resulting in warm and personalised hospitality. Diversity is an important consideration in our team as it embraces all cultures and has the ability to cater to various practices. We take the time and effort to understand a custom to make sure all that is required during an event is delivered with perfection. It is not just the spaces and banquet halls that deliver, but also the sense of joy that such events bring to our guests. All kinds of palates are tended to by our gifted chefs who cook up wonders in our fine dining restaurant, Hexa, as well as our happening watering hole, Casque.
